PyTexas 2013 Sponsors

PyTexas 2013 wouldn't be possible without the generous support of our sponsors, listed below.

Are you interested in sponsoring this year's PyTexas?

If you're interested in sponsoring, please review our Sponsor Prospectus. There are still a few amazing opportunities left. We'd love to add your name to this list! 

Platinum

MapMyFitness, Inc.

MapMyFitness is also our lanyard tag sponsor.

Headquartered in Austin, Texas, MapMyFitness is the health and fitness technology company that powers the Internet’s largest social network of fitness enthusiasts. The MapMyFitness suite of website and mobile applications, under the MapMyRun and MapMyRide flagship products, uses built-in GPS technology to provide users worldwide with the ability to map, record and share their exercise routes and workouts in an online database. In addition, users have access to a searchable database of over 120 million global routes, online training tools, nutrition tracking, fitness calculators, event listings, and the ability to easily share their activities with friends. For more information visit www.mapmyfitness.com.  

 

Rackspace

Rackspace Hosting (NYSE: RAX) is the open cloud company, delivering open technologies and powering more than 197,000 customers worldwide. Rackspace provides its renowned Fanatical Support® across a broad portfolio of IT products, including Public Cloud, Private Cloud, Hybrid Hosting and Dedicated Hosting. The company offers choice, flexibility and freedom from vendor lock in. Rackspace has been recognized by Bloomberg BusinessWeek as a Top 100 Performing Technology Company and is featured on Fortune’s list of 100 Best Companies to Work For. Rackspace was positioned in the Leaders Quadrant by Gartner Inc. in the "2011 Magic Quadrant for Managed Hosting." Rackspace is headquartered in San Antonio with offices and data centers around the world. For more information for developers, visit http://developer.rackspace.com.

 

Stormpulse

Stormpulse reduces weather risk for a broad range of industries including energy, transportation, defense, healthcare, and retail. Businesses and government agencies subscribe to Stormpulse to improve their long-range planning and daily decision-making surrounding the arrival and impact of significant weather events in the continental United States and Caribbean. 

 

HBK Capital Management

We (HBK Capital Management) are an alternative investment firm headquartered in Dallas and with offices in New York, London and Charlottesville. Technology is vital to all aspects of our investment operations. We code in Python, C++, C# and JavaScript to build trading systems and portfolio management applications. We are looking for talented, self-motivated and resourceful developers who love learning new technologies and solving hard problems. Send your resume – or even better – a sample of your work to careers@hbk.com.

Decisio

Decisio provides a clinical decision support solution, which initially will focus on the needs of care providers in trauma centers and intensive care units. Decisio Health provides an intuitive, consolidated view of the relevant patient information at the point of patient care. The software was created by clinicians, for clinicians and is currently utilized in one of the nations largest and most prestigious level 1 trauma centers.

 

Tendenci

Tendenci is Open Source Content Management Software for Non-Profit Organizations to manage their websites. Tendenci includes tools Nonprofits need like Memberships, Event Registration, and Photo & Video Galleries out-of-the-box. Tendenci is built on Python and Django, with a PostgreSQL database and was released with a GPL3 Open source license in 2012. Learn more at www.tendenci.com!
